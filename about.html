History Of Seaside Dew Cafe

A Taste of Tradition by the Seas

Our history

Founded in 2015 by passionate locals, Seaside Dew Café was born out of a love for both food and community. Located on the beautiful coast of Jamaica, our café has grown from a small seaside stall into a thriving hub for locals and visitors alike. From our humble beginnings, we’ve focused on quality, creativity, and a commitment to the local community


Our values

  • Community: We believe in creating a space where everyone feels welcome and valued.
  • Sustainability: Our commitment to using local, sustainable ingredients is at the heart of what we do.
  • Quality: We strive to serve only the finest dishes with a focus on flavor and freshness.
  • Creativity: Innovation in our menu is key—each dish tells a story.

Our goals

  • Expand our menu to include more vegan and gluten-free options.
  • Host monthly community events to connect with local customers.
  • Grow into a recognized brand that celebrates Jamaican cuisine internationally.
  • Implement a farm-to-table approach with our local suppliers.
